# Southern African Development Community Qualifications Framework (SADCQF)

## Member Countries

The SADCQF encompasses the 16 member states of the Southern African Development Community:
- Angola
- Botswana
- Comoros
- Democratic Republic of Congo
- Eswatini
- Lesotho
- Madagascar
- Malawi
- Mauritius
- Mozambique
- Namibia
- Seychelles
- South Africa
- Tanzania
- Zambia
- Zimbabwe
